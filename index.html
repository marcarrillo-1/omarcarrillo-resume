My name is Omar Carrillo Carrillo, I'm from Los Angeles California and I sign my work under the artist name Buddy. I find inspiration in many places and have traveled to Seattle,Mexico,Arizona,Japan which has influenced my creative style and process. I create art because I create art for my community as well as for entertainment purposes. To help express my emotions and to have a form of income by taking commissions by people who message me on my socials requesting anything they want. From traditional, on paper, art to digital art any type of art form is art. I specialize in Digital art. I use softwares such as paint tool sai with a Wacom Tablet, and procreate by apple with a 10inch Ipad and Apple Pencil and I focus on Anthropomorphism. I also enjoy working with Canson 5.5" X 8.5" sketchbook and copic markers. My art style is Cartoony with a bit of realism. I am motivated by Other artists and cartoons. I have been creating art for 5 years. My favorite artist is Ren/ Chammuco and I find their work to be Detailed, vibrant, and clean! I connect with the art community by posting my art on social media, joining art fights, and art trades with other artists. My work has been shown at Instagram. When people see my work, I hope they experience The learning processes it takes to draw and to learn and correct your mistakes along the way.

Being in love with art his whole life Omar Carrillo joined the art community in search of finding ways on how to improve and teach others on how to improve on their art. In 2015 I started my art journey and posted art ever since. I am a self taught artist I have learned from the basics of anatomy to color theory and lighting. From looking at other artists work I further mastred my craft and was able to create cleaner lineart,blend colors smoothly, and create art that inspires others.

Growing up in a poor household I started drawing with just simple supplies, a pencil and blank piece of paper. It wasn't easy at first because the art journey isn't easy. It is full of up's and down's. Over the years I embranced the beauty and culture of art. I began taking commissions around 2018 and by drawing a huge variety of characters, backrounds, and communicating with different diverse people I was motivated to further my artist career in order to find my true passion of drawing and what makes it such a beauty for the whole world to see. When people come across art its common for them to stand there for a minte or two to admire the detailed work the artist has created and why all these shapes, lines, and colors create such a mesmerizing and beautiful image. This is why I create art. There is no such thing as the right approach towards art. Just like with anything how Lao Tzu once said "A journey of a Thousand Miles Begins with a single step"
